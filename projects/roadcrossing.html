Road Crossing Assistant


Description Ongoing

An ongoing project with an aim to aid blind people for crossing road, more precisely Indian roads where there may not be many traffic signals, and where traffic rules and speed limits may not be followed strictly. For the same, we have collected a dataset of videos. Firstly, we plan to make a machine learning model and use this dataset to predict the safe duration for crossing road in the given video. Then, we plan to integrate this model into a wearable assistant so that blind people can use it for crossing roads in realtime.

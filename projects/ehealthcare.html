E Health Care


Description

E-healthcare deals with online medicine tracking and fitness monitoring of the public. It provides users the facility to set reminders regarding the intake of medicines. In addition, it also provides suggestions regarding the adequate intake of medicines. The fitness tracking feature takes the userâ€™s height, body and weight as an input to calculate the BMI (Body Mass Index) and suggests exercises to the user. An option of food diary is also given which recommends the user the calorie intake and diet suggestions. With the help of food diary, the user can increase or decrease their weight and use it to track their daily consumption.
